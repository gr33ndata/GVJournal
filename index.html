About us

The Global Voices Journal for Citizen Media is a multilingual, bi-annual, open access publication that aims at decentralizing the flow of information, by showcasing the work done by researchers not only in the main knowledge centers, but also from developing nations, working as a bridge between scholars all over the world.

The Global Voices Journal also introduces an innovative process of collaborative selection of articles to be published, with a direct choice from our readers and community members. Readers are given a period to vote for the most interesting articles on the comments section of each post. The most voted are published on the journal’s website. This way, each new issue is the result of the integration between academic work and participative media.
